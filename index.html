Our story

A family orchard in Rayong

Chut Thai started as three mangosteen trees behind our grandmother's kitchen. Three decades later, the orchard is bigger, but the rule hasn't changed: nothing goes on the trees that we wouldn't put on our own table.

Every basket is hand-sorted before it leaves the farm, so what you receive is exactly what we'd serve our own family.
